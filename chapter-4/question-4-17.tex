\[
    \frac{1}{5}
    \textbf{X}_{1}
    +
    \frac{1}{5}
    \textbf{X}_{2}
    +
    \frac{1}{5}
    \textbf{X}_{3}
    +
    \frac{1}{5}
    \textbf{X}_{4}
    +
    \frac{1}{5}
    \textbf{X}_{5}
\]
and
\[
    \textbf{X}_{1}
    -
    \textbf{X}_{2}
    +
    \textbf{X}_{3}
    -
    \textbf{X}_{4}
    +
    \textbf{X}_{5}
\]
in terms of $\bm{\mu}$ and $\bm{\Sigma}$. Also, obtain the covariance betweeen the two linear combinations of random vectors.
